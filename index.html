About

Hello, I am a Ph.D. candidate in Graduate School of Artificial Intelligence at Seoul National University. I am currently a member of IDS lab. My research interests are Natural Language Processing (NLP). In particular, I aim to develop reliable machine learning models capable of abstaining improper generations.
